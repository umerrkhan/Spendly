Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

To provide Spendly's core expense-tracking functionality, we collect the information you choose to enter into the app. This includes account details such as your name and email address, and the expense records you log — amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you add. We also collect basic technical information such as your browser type and the pages you visit within Spendly, used solely to keep the service running reliably.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to operate Spendly: to display your expenses, generate summaries and category breakdowns, secure your account, and respond to support requests. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your expense records for advertising. Aggregated, anonymous usage statistics may be used to improve the product, but these never identify you individually.

3. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of third-party services to function, such as hosting providers and, where applicable, email delivery services. These providers process data only on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy commitments. We do not share your expense records with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics platforms that profile individual users.

4. Data Retention and Your Choices

Your expense records are kept for as long as your account is active. You may edit or delete individual entries at any time, and you may delete your account entirely — when you do, your associated data is removed from our active systems. If you have any questions about exercising these choices, please get in touch using the details below.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled in Spendly, please reach out at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related queries within a reasonable timeframe.
